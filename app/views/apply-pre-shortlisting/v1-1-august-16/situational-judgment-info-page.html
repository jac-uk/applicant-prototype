Situational judgement test

You’ll have [45] minutes to answer [20] multiple choice questions of equal weight.

Each question will present a situation that you might face in the role you’ve applied for.

In each situation ,there's an issue or problem you need to resolve, with [5] possible answers. You’ll need to tick a box for the best and worst answer in each case. There are no marks for next best or next worst answer.

You’ll be assessed on your reading of each situation and ability to judge how appropriate the possible answers are, not on knowledge of rules or procedures.
